    QUANTIX AI: OPERATIONAL GUIDE

    1. THE ENSEMBLE ENGINE (How it thinks)

    When you click Execute, three separate Neural Networks analyze the asset simultaneously. A signal is only generated if all three models reach consensus.

    • LSTM (Time-Series): Analyzes the last 30 days of raw market data (Price, Volume, RSI, MACD) to predict tomorrow's mathematical close.
    • CNN (Vision): Converts the chart into a 2D image and uses Computer Vision to detect visual shapes (Head & Shoulders, Bull Flags).
    • FinBERT (Sentiment): Analyzes live news headlines to gauge human fear and greed.

    2. LIVE DATA AUTOMATION

    The system connects directly to Yahoo Finance (yfinance) on the backend. It bypasses old CSV files and fetches the literal up-to-the-minute live ticks for whatever asset you query.

    3. EXPLAINABLE AI (XAI)

    When the CNN detects a pattern, it doesn't just guess. It uses Grad-CAM heatmapping to calculate the exact coordinate bounds of the pattern (e.g., "Flag detected between Day 14 and Day 18"). This is passed to the LLM Advisor for translation.
